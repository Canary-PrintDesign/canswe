CANSWE was started on a dream.

Our 27 Retailers, over 10,000 Customers, and 40 years of business have moved us closer to reaching it.

We love our customers, and they love us.
CAN-SWE is a company with grit.
" One thing I learned during my 15-year career as a feller in the commercial logging industry was that good chainsaw garments were either overpriced, offered too little protection, or both!
